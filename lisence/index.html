Lisence

NFD’s “Flower Designer Qualification Examination” began in 1967 and has a history of over 50 years.
It aims to promote floral design to people who are familiar with and love flowers, and to improve their skills and knowledge.
Anyone who loves flowers can take up the challenge at any time.

  • Level 3 Flower Designer provides the foundation of
    skills and knowledge.
  • Level 2 Flower Designers can acquire sufficient skills
    and knowledge on top of the basics.
  • Level 1 Flower Designers will take lessons to cultivate
    their ability to think and create on their own, building
    on the basics acquired in Levels 3 and 2, and take on
    the challenge of taking the exam.

NFD’s “Flower Designer Qualification Examination” is an examination that considers the “vegetation” of flowers first and foremost.
Flowers that bloom naturally around us are gently considered on the table and incorporated into the theme of the exam.

About the Flower Designer
Qualification Examination System

This is an examination system established in 1967 in line with NFD’s business objective of “spreading floral culture.
For 50 years, the NFD has held a nationwide standardized examination with uniform standards and has produced a large number of successful candidates.
The Flower Designer Certification Examination is a step-by-step system of examinations, from Level 3 to Level 2 to Level 1. Students should take sufficient lessons in preparation for the exam before taking the exam.
